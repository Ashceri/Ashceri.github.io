Final Project

In this project we made an air hocker and ping pong mash where instead of moving the paddles back and forth the user just moves them side to side. The ball travels on its own side to side. When you play against the computer the computer follows the puck and will always hit the puck. Meaning the user won't get to win on the computer. When you play multiplayer there will be a user using w and s keys and a user using the up and down keys. The Score goes on for an infinite amount of wins and the screen won't restart into the ontro screen.
